Über nestfindet

The nestovest is started as an idea to make buying apartments easier specially for the young generation who usually lack enough liquidity to get a loan from the back and also willing to stay flexible. Nestovest is selected as of the 9 teams out of 22 ones at "Meisterklasse 2" program from Gründermotor. During this program it has pivoted to the idea of simplifying the process of finding an apartment for tenants and also finding a tenant for landlords. We believe this is a right step towards our long-term vision.

Nestovest is founded by Ali Ahari and Nima Mazroob. Ali is a Phd student of informatics at the University of Tübingen and Nima is a Phd student of geoinformatics at Karlsruhe University of Technology. Currently Nestovest team is under the mentorship of Eric Heintze from PioneerGeist and Elena Schön from Hochschule für Technik Stuttgart.

Für Genossenschaften

Bester Weg um Mieter zu finden und verwalten

Mieterauswal ist schwierig, und genau das macht nestfinder mit maschinellem Lernen und künstlicher Inteligent ganz professionel und einfach für Sie. Wir digitalisieren eure Vergaberichtlinien und finden die richtige Mietern für Ihre Objekte. Sie können Terminvereinbarungen online erledigen lassen, Dokumente anfordern und danach die Property Verwaltung führen.
